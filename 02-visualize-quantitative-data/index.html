The Air We Breathe

Are NYers breathing heathy air? The short answer is yes.

Based on the data provided by NYC OpenData , the overall air quality of New York City has been increasing since 2009. Almost all boroughs except Manhattan have been at healthy level for the past 10 years. By 2018, all boroughs saw signficant air quality improvement based on average annual PM 2.5 values.
